What this scanner does (and does not)

What it does

  • Reviews publicly accessible website elements
  • Highlights DPDP-relevant data collection points
  • Surfaces missing or weak privacy disclosures
  • Produces a structured, audit-friendly summary

What it does NOT do

  • Declare DPDP compliance or non-compliance
  • Replace legal or audit interpretation
  • Certify DPDP readiness

This is a pre-audit and audit-readiness tool, not an audit opinion.

What you receive

A short, structured DPDP pre-audit report covering:

  • Website overview and scope
  • Public data collection touchpoints
  • Privacy policy and disclosure observations
  • Consent and cookie signals (where applicable)
  • Obvious DPDP readiness gaps
  • Items requiring auditor judgement

Designed to be reusable across DPDP audit engagements and easy to explain to clients.
