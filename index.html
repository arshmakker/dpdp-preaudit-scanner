What this scanner does (and does not)

What it does

  • Scans public websites for DPDP & privacy red flags
  • Identifies data collection points and consent signals
  • Highlights missing or weak disclosures
  • Produces a structured, audit-friendly summary

What it does NOT do

  • Declare compliance or non-compliance
  • Replace legal interpretation
  • Certify DPDP readiness

This is a pre-audit and readiness tool, not an audit opinion.

What you receive

A short, structured report covering:

  • Website overview & scope
  • Data collection touchpoints identified
  • Privacy policy & disclosure observations
  • Consent and cookie signals (where applicable)
  • Obvious DPDP readiness gaps
  • Items requiring auditor judgement

Designed to be reusable across engagements and easy to explain to clients.

Why we're building this

This is an early validation effort. Goal is to reduce repetitive audit prep. Improve consistency across DPDP engagements. Feedback from auditors will shape the product.
